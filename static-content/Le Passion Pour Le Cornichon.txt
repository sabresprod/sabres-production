שם פרויקט: Le Passion Pour Le Cornichon
נוצר לשם היצירה 
קטגוריה: A.I Content Creation & Video Editing 


תיאור:This project is a playful nod to my early days creating with AI tools, born from the desire to walk the fine line between absolute luxury and complete absurdity. The goal was to draw the viewer in with a serious, highly aesthetic commercial—much like a high-end French perfume campaign. I built anticipation using ultra-realistic, mesmerizing frames at the beginning, only to abruptly shatter it by revealing a completely ridiculous and nonsensical product. This piece comically demonstrates how AI technology can craft a highly credible, elegant facade while simultaneously exposing the "fake" in a self-aware manner, proving you can achieve top-tier visuals without taking things too seriously.
