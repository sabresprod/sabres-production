שם פרויקט: DJ SAGI KARIV Summer Tour
לקוח: DJ SAGI KARIV
קטגוריה: A.I Content Creation & Video Editing 


תיאור:When a world-renowned DJ asks you to create the marketing assets for his international summer tour, you simply can't refuse. We kicked things off with a collaborative brainstorming process to translate the vibrant energy of his music into a striking and unique visual language, resulting in a campaign filled with highly creative, precise, and colorful deliverables. The project opens with an energetic teaser video that reveals the tour dates, instantly pulling the audience into a fun, summery vibe through rich graphics and refreshing elements. For the first leg of the tour, we broke the mold with original concepts: a design mimicking a premium collector's action figure of the DJ, alongside a poster series heavily inspired by the 90s club scene. By using bold, bright, and fluorescent neon colors, we crafted a visual identity that radiates electrifying energy and promises the crowd an absolutely unforgettable summer.
