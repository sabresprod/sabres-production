תיאור:As a content creator, one of the most exciting challenges is taking professional, informative, and sometimes even scientific information, and transforming it into a captivating visual story you simply can't take your eyes off. In this project, which brings together three distinct topics centered around health, nutrition, and lifestyle, the goal was to make the core messages as clear, aesthetic, and pleasing as possible.

By integrating advanced AI tools that I’ve deeply explored and mastered, I created custom B-roll footage for each video, tailored perfectly to its specific vibe and pacing. The vision was to maintain the clean, minimalist, and polished look that I highly value in this kind of work. This approach doesn't just visually support the speaker—it actively breaks down and simplifies complex concepts, from gut health and sleep techniques to cellular processes. The final result is a smart visual envelope that makes the entire viewing experience seamless, engaging, and remarkably accessible and human for the audience.
