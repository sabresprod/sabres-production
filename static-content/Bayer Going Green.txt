לקוח: Bayer
קטגוריה: A.I Content Creation & Video Editing

תיאור:When Bayer decided to take a significant step toward a greener future by replacing its employee fleet with electric vehicles, it was clear the announcement had to be nothing short of impressive. The main challenge I faced was distilling this major news into a tight timeframe of just 15 seconds, without losing any of the initiative's power and impact. To achieve this, I created a video that maintains a dynamic pace, a clean, cinematic look, and a sharp, clear visual message. The result is a concise piece that directly conveys the company's sense of progress and renewal, capturing attention from the very first moment and leaving the employees fully informed and excited about the upcoming change.
