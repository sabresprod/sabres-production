תיאור:When invited to my close friends' wedding, I wanted to create a unique video that highlights the funny side of their relationship to break the ice and ease the natural tension. The challenge was engaging a crowd that intimately knows the couple, meaning the content had to be spot-on, genuinely funny, and never boring. I started by asking them three specific questions designed to spark surprising and candid answers. By combining these authentic human moments with clever editing and the power of AI, the final result effortlessly came to life as an unforgettable, upbeat, and hilarious wedding video.
